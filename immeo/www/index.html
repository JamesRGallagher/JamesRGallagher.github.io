ABOUT IMMEO

What Is Immeo?

Immeo is the new way to pay for club entry. Simply signup to your city, download & verify the app, then show your verified status to any of the partipating clubs & enjoy your night!

Immeo is not restiricted. As long as you are within the venue's guidelines, you can come an go as you please, all night!

How It Works

When you signup for £10 per month, you get unlimited access to any venue listed within your city, on any day, regardless of entry fee.

Got friends in another club? No probem!

Low on funds? No problem!

Fancy trying somewhere new? No problem!

App Updates

Over time, the app will be updated with the latest events for your area, special drinks deals, photography from events & subscriber only invites to private parties around the UK!
