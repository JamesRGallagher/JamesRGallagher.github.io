Eventzee makes it easy to create your own scavenger hunt for work get-togethers, family outings, and much more. Whether it’s for 200 people or 2, our feature-filled and user-friendly admin tools will get your game going in no time.

When you host an eventzee event, the choice is yours. Choose between a traditional photo-based scavenger hunt or a QR code-based hunt, where players use Eventzee to locate QR code stickers hidden in the wild. Determine winners based on timed hunts or points gained from scanning codes, or snapping the correct photos.

Make the most of your hunt with Eventzee’s high-powered admin tools to keep you in full control of the game. Approve photos, reward bonus points and even change the game up with real-time direct player messaging. Blast message all the players at once or privately message one- the choice is yours.
