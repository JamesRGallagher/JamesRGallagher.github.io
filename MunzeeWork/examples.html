Still not convinced?

Eventzee may have just launched in January 2015, but our new app is forged from more than three years of special events run via the Munzee Scavenger Hunt app. Since Munzee’s inception in July 2011 we have experimented, revised, tried, tried again, started over, added, subtracted, tried again, and then perfected the special event scavenger hunt. The outcome was Eventzee, a stand alone app powered by Munzee. The proof is in the pudding with 200,000 players and over 2.5 million gamepieces in play, Munzee knows how to operate a large scale worldwide scavenger hunt. Eventzee is operated by the same team- based in McKinney, Texas- and we are excited to offer the success of Munzee’s events to your company, organization or school!
